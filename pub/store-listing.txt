Full description:
This app adds Fraser Valley Express (BC Transit) bus information to MonTransit BETA.

This app provides the bus schedule and news from YouTube and @BCTransit on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

FVX bus serve Abbotsford, Chilliwack, Langley in British Columbia, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display bus information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by BC Transit.
http://bctransit.com/*/footer/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-fraser-valley-express-bus-android

This app is not related with BC Transit and Central Fraser Valley Transit System.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from YouTube and Twitter
